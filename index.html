Marinated Egg Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup Soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup Rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Water
  • 1 small piece of ginger (optional)

Instructions

1. Boil the eggs for 7-8 minutes until hard-boiled.

2. Prepare the marinade by mixing soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, and water in a bowl.

3. Peel the eggs and place them in the marinade for at least 2 hours.

4. Serve the marinated eggs with your favorite dishes or as a snack!
